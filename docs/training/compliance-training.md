# Compliance Training Materials

This document provides comprehensive training materials for the Jackdaw Sentry compliance system, designed to help users understand and effectively utilize all compliance features.

### Training Objectives
- Understand the EU regulatory framework (GDPR, DORA, MiCA, AMLR)
- Master compliance workflows and case management
- Learn risk assessment and regulatory reporting procedures
- Develop skills in audit trail management and data protection
- Gain proficiency in compliance analytics and reporting

### Target Audience
- **Compliance Officers**: Primary users responsible for regulatory compliance
- **Investigators**: Users conducting crypto investigations
- **Risk Analysts**: Professionals performing risk assessments
- **Management**: Executives overseeing compliance programs
- **IT Administrators**: System administrators managing compliance infrastructure

- **Basic Course**: 2 hours

## üéì Module 1: Regulatory Framework Fundamentals

### 1.1 EU Regulations Overview

#### GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand GDPR principles and requirements
- Learn data protection by design and by default
- Master data subject rights and breach notification

**Key Concepts:**
- **Data Minimization**: Collect only necessary data
- **Purpose Limitation**: Use data only for specified purposes
- **Storage Limitation**: Keep data only as long as necessary
- **Accountability**: Demonstrate compliance through documentation

**Practical Exercise:**
1. Identify personal data in compliance workflows
2. Implement data minimization principles
3. Create data retention policies
4. Practice breach notification procedures

#### DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand DORA requirements for operational resilience
- Learn incident reporting and management procedures
- Master business continuity and disaster recovery

**Key Concepts:**
- **Operational Resilience**: Ability to withstand disruptions
- **Incident Management**: Detection, response, and recovery
- **Business Continuity**: Essential functions during disruptions
- **Digital Security**: Protection of digital assets

**Practical Exercise:**
1. Conduct operational resilience assessment
2. Develop incident response procedures
3. Create business continuity plans
4. Test disaster recovery procedures

#### MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand MiCA requirements for crypto-asset service providers
- Learn market integrity and consumer protection measures
- Master supervisory reporting obligations

**Key Concepts:**
- **CASP Compliance**: Crypto-Asset Service Provider requirements
- **Market Integrity**: Fair and orderly markets
- **Consumer Protection**: Investor safeguards
- **Supervisory Reporting**: Regulatory reporting obligations

**Practical Exercise:**
1. Assess CASP compliance requirements
2. Implement market integrity measures
3. Create consumer protection procedures
4. Practice supervisory reporting

#### AMLR (Anti-Money Laundering Regulation)
**Learning Objectives**
- Understand AMLR requirements for financial institutions
- Learn transaction monitoring and suspicious activity detection
- Master SAR generation and submission procedures

**Key Concepts:**
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Real-time suspicious activity detection
- **Risk Assessment**: ML-powered risk scoring
- **SAR Generation**: Suspicious Activity Report creation
- **Customer Due Diligence**: Enhanced investigation procedures

**Practical Exercise:**
1. Implement transaction monitoring procedures
2. Conduct risk assessments
3. Generate SAR reports
4. Practice customer due diligence
